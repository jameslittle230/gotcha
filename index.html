The point of Gotcha is to tag as many students on campus as possible without being tagged yourself.

This program sets up everyone with a tag code, which is a unique identifier that people should keep secret. Each person also has a target. The program is designed to randomly assign targets in a loop, so each person will have a unique target throughout the entire game. When you tag your target, your target gives you his tag code. When you enter the tag code into the website, it marks the target as tagged and gives you your new target.
